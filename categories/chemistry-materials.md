# Chemistry and Materials Workflow Tools

This category tracks chemistry, docking, cheminformatics, and materials-science tools that may become future OpenSciFlow plugin/workflow examples.

The first OpenSciFlow implementation should remain protein-computing focused. Chemistry and materials expansion should come after the initial protein workflow works.
